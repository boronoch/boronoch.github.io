Who We Are

Brothers of Faith is a Small Faith Group for Young Adult Men in the Milwaukee area.

The group meets weekly on Wednesdays at 7:30 pm at the home of one of the members.

The group’s discussion rotates through Scripture reading, Catechism study, and discussion of current events or cultural issues.

Each discussion is led by one or two members of the group, and new topics for the coming month are chosen in the third week of every month.

What We're Doing

Brothers of Faith meets Wednesday evenings from 7:30 – 9:30 PM.
